VOLUNTEERS

Volunteers are the heart of our shelter. People come from all over the world to volunteer - bringing love, hope and help to our animals. Some people stay for an hour or a day and others return year after year. Volunteers help clean, build and repair houses and fences, walk and cuddle the animals. Everything a volunteer does improves the shelter and the lives of our animals. Whether you're coming for a few hours or a few weeks, we guarantee that volunteering at Animal Shelter Marjan will be a unique and unforgettable experience and we welcome you to join us! Check out our contact page to join us!

DOG WALKING


Our dogs become more adoptable when they spend more time with people. Taking them for walks is a great way to help them be ready to meet a potential new owner.

FEEDING ANIMALS


There is usually only 2 of us at the shelter and we have the massive task of feeding and cleaning up after hundreds of animals every day. We appreciate any help we can get.

PHOTOGRAPHY


Our animals can't get adopted without great photos to show off how amazing they are! We are desperately seeking photographers to volunteer their time and expertise to take great photos of our animals so we can share their gorgeous faces with new, potential owners.

TRANSPORT


Our animals regularly require visits to the vet for desexing or other vet procedures. We rely on volunteers to transport them so we can focus on feeding and looking after over 300 animals at our shelter. If you have a car and are available to help, we would love to hear from you!
